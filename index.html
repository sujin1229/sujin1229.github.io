Sujin Jin

M.S. Student, Dept. of Global Smart City, Sungkyunkwan University · SCIT Lab (Advisor: Prof. Seunghee Park)

Smart Construction IT (SCIT) Lab

Sungkyunkwan University, Suwon, Republic of Korea

I am a master’s student in the Department of Global Smart City at Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU), working in the Smart Construction IT (SCIT) Lab with Prof. Seunghee Park. I completed my B.Eng. in Civil, Architectural Engineering and Landscape Architecture at SKKU (summa cum laude).

My research builds AI-driven systems for construction and infrastructure safety, at the intersection of computer vision, large language models, and 3D reconstruction. Current threads include multi-CCTV 3D worker localization with UAV-based scene reconstruction, LLM-guided autonomous robot navigation in ROS 2 / Gazebo, and multimodal non-destructive testing (NDT) for infrastructure diagnostics. My first-author work on explainable, GPT-4-assisted concrete-defect diagnosis appears in Automation in Construction.

I try to keep a “field-first” (현장 우선) stance: technology should return to the practitioners on site — closing the loop between the field, the research, and the field again. I am currently applying to PhD programs in the United States to continue this work.
